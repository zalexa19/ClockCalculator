About the time calculator

This calculator allows you to calculate how many days, hours and minutes there are between two dates.

Instructions

Automatically, the duration calculator is populated with today's date and current hour.

  1. Choose the starting time
  2. Choose the starting date (optional)
  3. Choose the ending time
  4. Choose the ending date
  5. Press 'Calculate'

Uses of time duration calculator

Time calculators have a lot of different and interesting uses

  • intermittent fasting - calculate how many hours passed since the last meal
  • Calculate how many days you have been on a new diet
  • Calculate how many hours you slept
  • Calculate how many hours you worked on a project
  • Calculate how many days are left until a specific date
